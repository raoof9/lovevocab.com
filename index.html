The technique

This isn't just another flashcard app

It's built on two ideas learning science keeps proving work: active recall and spaced repetition. LoveVocab automates both, so all you do is answer honestly.

1

AI does the hard part

Paste a word, a phrase, or a topic. AI instantly structures a full flashcard — definition, examples, translation, common mistakes, whatever fits the subject. No manual data entry, ever.

2

The Leitner system schedules your memory

Every card lives in one of 5 boxes with increasing review gaps — 1, 2, 4, 8, 16 days. Answer right, it moves up a box. Answer wrong, it drops back to box 1.

3

You only review what's due

No overwhelming decks, no busywork. Open the app and you'll see exactly the cards your brain is about to forget — nothing more.

Questions

Frequently asked

Is this just another flashcard app?

No. Most flashcard apps still make you write every card by hand and decide for yourself what to study. LoveVocab uses AI to build the card and the Leitner system to decide exactly what's due — you just answer.

Does it only work for language learning?

Not at all. Vocabulary and phrases are one use case, but the same engine works for programming concepts, system design notes, definitions for an exam — anything you'd otherwise put on a flashcard.

What exactly is the Leitner system?

A spaced-repetition method using 5 boxes with increasing review intervals — 1, 2, 4, 8 and 16 days. A correct answer moves a card to the next box, so you see it less often. A wrong answer sends it back to box 1, so it comes back sooner. Over time you stop wasting time on what you already know.

Is my data private?

Yes — completely. LoveVocab has no account system, no backend server, and no analytics. Every card and every review lives only on your device. See the Privacy Policy for details.

When is it launching?

LoveVocab is in development for iOS and coming to the App Store soon. There's no email list or signup — just check back here.

Do I need to create an account?

No. There's nothing to sign up for. Open the app and start adding cards.
